Say It With Sweets
===================
Let us help make your next event special by creating a cake, cupcakes or cookies to match your theme. We also offer our famous gourmet cupcakes for any occasion or just because you want some.. Be sure to browse our website for gifts such as cookie bouquets, chocolate-covered treats or cookie trays, certain to delight anyone. Please order early to make sure you don't miss out on any of our "sweet" creations.

"Sweet" gifts for everyone or for your special celebration..we have you covered..cakes, cupcakes, cookies.

Custom made cookies and cupcakes must be ordered at least 7 working days in advance...regular and gourmet cupcakes at least 1-2 working days in advance...cookie cakes and cookie trays - at least 5 working days in advance..please note..please order as early as possible ...during very busy weeks we may not be able to accommodate you..
