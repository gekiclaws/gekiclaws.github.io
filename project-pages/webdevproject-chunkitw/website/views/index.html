Welcome to Say It With Sweets!

What we do

Say it with Sweets is a bakery that provides cakes, cookies, cupcake creations for all occasions, and offers unique sweet gifts. Located in Altoona, Pennsylvania, we've been in business for over 12 years.

Our story

Say it With Sweets was started in 2012 by Sharon Ebersole. With a passion for baking and making people smile, Sharon set out to create a bakery that could provide sweet treats for any occasion.
