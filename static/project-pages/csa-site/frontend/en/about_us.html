Company Profile

Baidu's largest partner in overseas markets

Values

We encourage our employees to strive towards our 6 core values: innovation, commitment, love, integrity, collaboration, and knowledgeable.

Our Mission

As the exclusive advertising agency in Hong Kong, Macau and Singapore, and owning advertising agency rights in Australia, the United States, the United Kingdom, France, Cyprus and other regions, we hope to provide advertisers in these markets with effective Baidu advertising solutions to help them successfully explore the Chinese market.

Our Services

Our services include a variety of different advertising value-added services, such as advertising optimization, performance tracking, and data analysis, to comprehensively assist advertisers to further enhance advertising effectiveness.
